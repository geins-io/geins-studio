# What is Geins Merchant Center?

## Introduction

Geins Merchant Center is a comprehensive administrative interface designed for e-commerce applications. This standalone, headless application seamlessly integrates your Geins PIM, CRM, WMS, CMS, and more. Its flexible architecture allows integration with any e-commerce platform, enhancing your business's operational efficiency.

## Features

- **Ready to use**: Geins Merchant Center comes out of the box fully integrated with the Geins suite.

- **Fully customizable**: Tailor everything to your or your clients needs.

- **Headless**: Geins Merchant Center is a headless application, allowing you to integrate it with any e-commerce platform.

## Built with

Geins Merchant Center is built with **Nuxt.js**, **shadcn-vue** an **Tailwind CSS**, making it fully extendable and customizable.
